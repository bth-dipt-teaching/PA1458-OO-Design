* About This Seminar
  The purpose of this seminar is to discuss common issues identified while marking Release 1, and to further discuss any learning opportunities that you may have identified.

  *NOTE:* If you have any disagreements with how the assignment was marked please contact the teacher separately /after/ this seminar to book a meeting where you may discuss your solution and the received feedback in detail.

* Preparations Before the Seminar
  - Submit the release for marking.
  - Reflect upon the received feedback and make note of comments where you like to find out more.
* Seminar Agenda
  1. Quick rundown of the Assignment (with brief examples)
  2. Discussion of Common Issues
  3. General Discussion
